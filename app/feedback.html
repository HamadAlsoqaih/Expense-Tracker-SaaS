Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your expense data in CSV, PDF, or Excel formats from the Reports section in your dashboard.

Is there a mobile app?

Our platform is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. We're also developing native mobile apps.

How secure is my financial data?

We use bank-level encryption and are SOC 2 Type II compliant. Your data is never shared with third parties.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. No cancellation fees apply.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. Contact support for refund requests.
